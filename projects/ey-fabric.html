The Role

The only practitioner on the team closing the full loop — from behavioral signal to Figma.

At EY Fabric I'm embedded as the sole Pendo practitioner on the XDA team — the link between what the platform does, what users experience, and what product and engineering prioritize next. No handoffs between disciplines. One person, one loop.

Sole Pendo practitioner on the XDA team — I own all instrumentation, funnels, journeys, paths, segments, and reports across 90+ marketplace assets.
UXR on-demand: 1:1 interviews, Maze tests, Optimal Workshop sessions, and Pendo in-app surveys and walkthroughs — triggered by behavioral signals, not scheduled sprints.
Synthesize analytics + research using Copilot connected to EY's SharePoint source of truth, then deliver actionable design proposals via Figma Make with the design system already connected.
When findings reveal bugs or critical friction — output prioritized tickets to product and development via ADO, with behavioral evidence attached.
When design is needed, I'm part of that loop too — Figma proposals following the existing design system, auto layout, and component guidelines. Code-ready, behavioral evidence on every frame.
