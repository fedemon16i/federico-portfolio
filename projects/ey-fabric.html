The Role

The only role at the intersection of behavior, research, and design

Sole Pendo practitioner on the XDA team — I own all instrumentation, funnels, journeys, paths, segments, and reports across 100+ marketplace assets.
Run UXR on-demand and proactively: 1:1 interviews, Maze tests, Optimal Workshop sessions, and Pendo in-app surveys and walkthroughs — triggered by behavioral signals, not scheduled sprints.
Synthesize analytics + research using Copilot connected to EY's SharePoint source of truth, then deliver actionable design proposals via Figma Make with the design system already connected.
When findings reveal bugs, performance issues, or critical friction — output prioritized tickets to product and development via ADO, with behavioral evidence attached.
Collaborate with UI and visual designers who audit and refine Figma Make proposals — I provide the evidence trail and the first proposal; they bring the visual craft.
How I Work

A system where every design decision has a behavioral evidence trail

Analytics and research don't happen in sequence — they run in parallel, on-demand and proactively, triggered by what the platform surfaces. The output is always one of two things: a design proposal or a prioritized ticket.
